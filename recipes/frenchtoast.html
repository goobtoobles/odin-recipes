French Toast

Description

French toast is among the most classic of breakfasts. You'll find this in restaurants all over. I like to have some sausage links on the side.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 8 slices of thick, day-old bread
  • 3 tbps butter, plus more for serving
  • Optional toppings:
    • powdered sugar
    • maple syrup
    • berries
    • whipped cream

Steps

  1. Beat together eggs, milk, cream, salt and vanilla into a custard
  2. Put bread slices in a large baking dish and pour over custard. Keep flipping and moving the bread around until it soaks up all of the mixture
  3. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat. In batches, cook slices 2 to 3 minutes each side until golden brown
  4. Serve with desired toppings
